North Korea Missile Range

Mon 11 Sep 2017, 11:05 AM AEST

Below we will have a look at a map of the world and see just how far North Korean missiles can reach around the globe. Scary stuff!

North Korea's has been building intercontinental ballistic missiles which can be loaded with nuclear warheads. In the past the missiles have been limited in range. But this is changing recently.

As you can see now the top part of Australia is in range.

Most of Alaska is also currently in range.
